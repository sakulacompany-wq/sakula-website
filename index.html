About Us

A proudly South African company

Sakula General Trading is a 100% black-owned company registered with the CIPC on 25 September 2015 and headquartered in Kutlwanong, Kimberley. Led by Chief Executive Officer Mr. Samuel Wesi Mogotsi, we provide honest, realistic and reliable service on every contract we take on.

Our directors stay hands-on and in control of the key functions on every project. Clear communication and follow-through ensure our clients' needs come first in the planning and execution of all our work — from guarding a single site to securing facilities across ten towns at once.

Beyond protection, we exist to create jobs for the unemployed, help eradicate poverty, and play an active role in building our country's economy through sustainable services.
